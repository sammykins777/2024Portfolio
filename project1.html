Typography Art

This project draws inspiration from Jan Tschichold, one of the preeminent typographers of the 20th century. The typography and art direction were influenced by his perspective on the necessity of asymmetry in design, as articulated in his notable quote. The text layout is an article that delves into Tschichold's illustrious career.
